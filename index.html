Elzero Web School / Youtube Channel

Elzero Web School is a YouTube channel whose first goal is to find a simple way to create the largest technology community with a group of developers.

Elzero Web School is the largest Arabic channel in the world to teach the necessary programming content to developers. It was established in 2004 by "Osama El-Zero". It has a wide range of content and all the necessary forks for any developer to start in the software field.

Osama El-Zero said in one of the interviews that the channel’s goal is education only and not profit, and the profit that comes from the channel belongs to the subscribers because it is their right.
